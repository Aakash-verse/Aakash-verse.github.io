About

PhD student (expected 2025–2028) — CNRS / INRIA / COATI / 3IA / Université Côte d’Azur, Nice

My background is in physics (BS–MS, IISER Kolkata) and I work on problems at the intersection of machine learning, theoretical computer science and physics. Specifically, my interests include Hopfield networks and brain inspired learning dynamics, theory of neural network pruning and neural scaling laws.
